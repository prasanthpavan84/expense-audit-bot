# ADR 0001: Record Architecture Decisions

## Status
Approved

## Context
We need a structured way to document key architectural choices, rationales, and design trade-offs made during the redesign of the Expense Audit Bot. This ensures alignment, maintains historical context, and helps reviewers understand our technical decisions.

## Decision
We will adopt the standard Architecture Decision Record (ADR) format. All major architectural decisions will be recorded as markdown files inside `docs/adr/`, numbered sequentially.

## Consequences
- Every major design decision is documented, transparent, and reviewable.
- Future maintainers can trace decisions back to their original constraints and rationales.
